Our Story

Born from the underground streets of Punjab, Harkar Clothing is a rebellion against mass-produced fashion. We believe that streetwear is more than just clothes—it's a second skin. Every garment in our collection is 100% handmade, featuring heavy-weight premium cotton and meticulous stitching. From our first boxy hoodie to our technical cargos, we stay true to our roots: Premium Quality, Ethical Craftsmanship, and Unmatched Comfort. Join the movement.
